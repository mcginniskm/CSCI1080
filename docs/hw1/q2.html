Subjects in Economics

Some of the main fields within the economics discipline.


Microeconomics

Microeconomics is concerned with market interactions as the individual or firm level.

Macroeconomics

Macroeconomics deals with the study of a nation's economy as a whole.

Econometrics

Econometrics is a field which describes economics phenomenon using math/statistical methods.

This is just a short list. Economics has many subfields.
